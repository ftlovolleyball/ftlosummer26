Safe Sport, Parent Conduct & Spectator Expectations

FTLO is committed to creating a respectful and safe youth sport environment. Players, parents, guardians, spectators, staff, volunteers, and contractors are expected to support a positive tournament experience.

Parent/Guardian and Spectator Expectations:

  • Treat all youth participants, opponents, officials, volunteers, and FTLO staff with respect.
  • Do not badger, criticize, intimidate, or confront youth participants, including players from other teams.
  • Do not interfere with games, officiating, scorekeeping, or tournament operations.
  • Give players enough space to run, chase balls, warm up, and play safely.
  • Stay outside designated playing areas unless invited by FTLO staff.
  • Follow directions from FTLO tournament staff at all times.

FTLO may remove any parent, guardian, spectator, or participant whose behaviour is unsafe, disruptive, disrespectful, or harmful to the youth sport environment. No refund will be issued in such cases.

Safe Sport: FTLO staff, contractors, and volunteers should avoid being alone one-on-one with a youth participant in a private or isolated setting. Interactions with youth should remain observable, interruptible, and connected to the tournament.

Health & Communicable Illness

FTLO asks that all participants take personal responsibility for their health before attending the event.

  • Do not attend if unwell. If your child is experiencing symptoms of any contagious illness (fever, cough, vomiting, diarrhea, or other communicable symptoms), they must not participate.
  • Self-screening is required. You confirm that to the best of your knowledge, your child is not currently ill and has not been directed to isolate by a healthcare provider.
  • Notify FTLO promptly if your child develops a contagious illness within 48 hours of the event at operations@ftlovolleyball.ca.
Code of Conduct & Check-In / Check-Out

Your child is expected to conduct themselves respectfully and safely throughout the event. This includes treating all other participants, opponents, and event staff with respect; refraining from harassment or threatening behaviour; respecting the facility; and following instructions from FTLO staff at all times.

Participants must check in at the FTLO tournament command tent before playing and must check out if leaving before the end of the event.

Facility & Weather Conditions

This is an outdoor event at Thomas Kidd Elementary Park. It is not operated by the school or school district. Participants must respect all posted venue rules and public space expectations.

The tournament will run unless FTLO determines conditions are unsafe. Because outdoor weather can change quickly, same-day changes may occur. FTLO will make weather-related decisions as early as reasonably possible.

Participants should come prepared: sunscreen, water, weather-appropriate clothing, and footwear suitable for grass volleyball are strongly recommended.

Weather-related cancellation, credit, refund, or reschedule options will follow the tournament registration terms.
