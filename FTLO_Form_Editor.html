How to use

  1. Click ↑ Load and select your index.html from your downloads
  2. Use the left menu — ★ starred sections are most frequently updated
  3. Make edits — live previews update as you type
  4. Click ✓ Apply then ↓ Download
  5. Upload the downloaded file to GitHub — live in ~60 seconds
